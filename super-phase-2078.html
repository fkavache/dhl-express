Super Phase 2078

The work begins in stillness — a single frame held long enough to reveal its own instability. Shot on Agfa Movexoom 3000, the first scenes unfold through layers of grain and light bleed, where colour is not a property of objects but a memory of exposure. The film opens with an image of a hand pressed flat against an unmarked surface, and it is never fully clear whether the hand is pressing in or pressing out.

Metamorphosis here is not triumphant. It is slow, grinding, and often indistinguishable from decay. The Zenit Krasnogorsk-3 was used for sequences shot under fluorescent light — the flicker of the bulbs absorbed into the frame rate, so that what you see and what the film records diverge in a barely perceptible pulse. Bodies move through spaces that feel recently abandoned or not yet inhabited. The distinction collapses.

Between shots, there are moments of near-abstraction — the film stock itself becomes the subject, its chemical surface buckled by heat or over-exposure into patterns that read as landscape, as skin, as something without a name. These passages were not planned. They emerged in the developing process and were kept precisely because they could not be repeated. The work holds them as evidence of something that happened and cannot be undone.

The second part of the work shifts register. Where the first part accumulates texture, the second strips it away — longer takes, fixed camera positions, subjects who do not perform awareness of the camera but whose stillness suggests a negotiation happening just outside the frame. The two parts were edited separately and shown together, so that the seam between them remains visible and unresolved.

Renewal is present in the work but it is not offered as consolation. The final sequences return to the image of the hand — now lifted, now suspended — and what follows is less a resolution than a re-entry into the same conditions the film began with. The cycle is not closed. It simply starts again, slightly altered, carrying forward everything that came before it without explanation.

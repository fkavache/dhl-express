Nadyroba

Nadyroba ("Nadyroba Tsarsul Droshi") asks one essential question: "መን ንመን ይሃድን ኣሎ?", which translates from Tigrinya as "Who is hunting whom?".

Nadyroba is the act of searching for, following, and catching a target. Nadyroba is a one time act - either you catch the target or you don’t. Either your mind mogynadirebs, or you moynadireb your mind.

The artwork shows three different "momnadyrebeli" and three different "mosanadyrebeli". They are all the same, shown in different states of tension, like the tension of a drawn bow.

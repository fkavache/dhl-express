Super Phase 2078

About

Super Phase 2078 is a two-part analogue artwork and short film shot on super 8mm and 16mm films, made up of scenes that slowly come together into a single reflection on metamorphosis. Across both parts, the work explores transformation not as a smooth, straightforward change, but as a break—biological, spiritual, emotional, and ⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠. It is a look at how existence cracks open, rebuilds itself, and reveals new forms through cycles of decay, ⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠, and renewal. The film was shot between 2024 and 2025 using Agfa Movexoom 3000 and Zenit Krasnogorsk-3 film cameras.

Phase 2078

About

Phase 2078 is a three-part digital artwork that explores how the pursuit of understanding destabilizes identity. Rather than presenting existence as smooth, vivid, or beautiful, it portrays it as rough, noisy, and pale. It begins with a single nail piercing the brain, expands into a series of nails, and finally concludes with their extraction.

Nadyroba

About

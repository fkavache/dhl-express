Super Phase 2078

About

Super Phase 2078 is a two-part analogue artwork and short film shot on super 8mm and 16mm films, made up of scenes that slowly come together into a single reflection on metamorphosis. Across both parts, the work explores transformation not as a smooth, straightforward change, but as a break—biological, spiritual, emotional, and ⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠⊠. It is a look at how existence cracks open, rebuilds itself, and reveals new forms through cycles of decay, suffering, and renewal.
